About us

who we are

Adventure Trails Hikes is a dedicated pationate team offering extreme hiking in a number of different countries.

we offers supervised hikes, ranging from short day tours to weeklong treks, with camping. The service is aimed to both amateurs and professionals

our mission

our focus is not just on the experience of nature but also the specific nation's culture and traditions.

Hike experiences will typically include traditional meals, music/theatre, as well as contact with the local population. Additionally, the emphasis is on sustainable, environmentally behaviour and respect for the local culture.
