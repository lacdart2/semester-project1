Adventure Trail Hikes

Welcome to Adventure Trails Hikes!

We aim to offer the most exciting, challenging, educational, and cultural extreme hikes, in exhilarating locations around the globe.

We have a variety of standard packages, as well as offering specialized tours that will cater to your specific requirements.

our hikes durations

Our short hikes are perfect for weekend getaways or teambuilding trips. Normally these are day trips from a local base, and do not require camping equipment.

Camping hikes are longer than short hikes and include 3 or more nights camping.
You may bring your own camping equipment, or we can supply it for you.

These are the most extreme of all of our extreme hikes, and are usually two weeks or longer.
They include trekking, local culture and food, as well as nature excursions.

Our Famous destinations

Norway

Norway is an incredible place to explore, with untamed mythical landscapes, mountains, valleys, and fjords. our hicking area is just outside the gateway to the Fjord

Morocco

M’Goun Massif is not the tallest mountain in Morocco, but the surrounding area is one of the more beautiful areas of the Atlas Mountains. Picturesque and physically challenging.

India

Some of the most breathtaking views of Northern India are included on this three-week tour, with an increasingly demanding hiking itinerary.
